Our Story

Our Journey is Built on Trust & Innovation

Founded in 2011, Qbit Technologies started as a small team of passionate technologists with a vision to democratize enterprise-grade IT solutions. What began as a local IT support company has grown into a full-service technology partner serving hundreds of businesses across Nepal and beyond.

Our name, "Qbit," draws inspiration from the quantum bit — the fundamental unit of quantum computing — representing our belief that even the smallest innovations can drive transformative change. Today, we continue to push boundaries, leveraging the latest technologies to solve complex business challenges.

We believe technology should be an enabler, not a barrier. That's why every solution we deliver is designed with simplicity, scalability, and security at its core.
